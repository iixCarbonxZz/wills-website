Facials


Every facial treatment at Beauty on the Green is tailored to your skin. To ensure the right treatment for you all of our relaxing facials include an examination of your skin to determine its properties. While no two facials are the same, they all have common features;
  • A facial cleanse to remove oil, dirt and other debris
  • Exfoliation to take away dead skin
  • Steaming to clear pores, aid circulation and hydrate
  • A face mask to remove excess oil, close the pores and soften the skin
  • Finally, while the mask works its magic, a neck face and shoulder massage

    • We also offer additional treatments to perfectly enhance your facial experience including LED Light Therapy and Skin Peels.
    Light Emitting Diode (LED) phototherapy is the application of light energy to your skin for therapeutic benefit. The energy is used to improve cellular performance. LED therapy is known for its healing and anti-inflammatory properties. Research has shown that phototherapy can; Increase circulation, accelerate tissue repair, decrease wrinkles, decrease inflammation, improve skintones, ease muscle and joint pain and kill acne bacteria. Recommended as a course of 6 sessions for maximum results.

    A chemical peel is a high strength skin exfoliator. At Beauty on the Green, we offer a variety of chemical peels which come at different depths (Medium and Deep). Each peel uses a different chemical solution and pH level.

    Lashes and Brows



    Brows


    At Beauty on the Green we offer eyebrow and eyelash tinting using a colour suited to you to create a natural and balanced finish. We recommend complimenting your tint with our eyebrow shaping services which use either wax or threading to achieve your desired look. For the most complrehensive treatment, Highly Defined brows are for you. They create a complete look using tinting, waxing, threading and tweezing.

    Lashes


    Eyelash extensions are a way to extend the length and thickness of your natural lashes. Not to be confused with self stick strip false lashes, our semi-permanent extensions are adhered one by one on to the natural lashes using a specifically formulated adhesive that lasts a number of weeks. Our extensions are very comfortable to wear and are so lightweight that you wont even be able to feel you're wearing them, they look and feel completely natural.
