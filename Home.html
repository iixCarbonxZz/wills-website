About our Beauty Salon


Beauty on the Green is a boutique salon specialising in Facials, LED light therapy, Nails, Brows and Lashes. Conveniently located in the heart of the village, we are a fully qualified and insured salon offering high quality treatments for your beauty needs in a relaxed and friendly atmosphere.






Being a local salon allows us to take the time to get to know each of our clients, offering bespoke services suited to every individual. We offer complimentary consultations for all new clients and always ensure that each booking has enough time so that you can ask any questions you may have.
